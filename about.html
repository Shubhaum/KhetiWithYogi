About the work

Field experience, practical planning and farmer-first guidance.

KhetiWithYogi is built for farmers who want clear, local and usable guidance in protected cultivation instead of confusing theory.

Why KhetiWithYogi

Consultancy shaped by real crop cycles.

The consultant has complete hands-on experience cultivating crops including cucumber, tomato, broccoli, cauliflower, capsicum, bell pepper, bottle gourd, marigold, strawberry and coriander.

The focus is on decisions farmers face every week: which crop to plant, how to manage water, when to apply nutrition, how to protect plants, how to plan labour and how to sell with better confidence.

Local Fit

Advice is shaped for Ajmer, Pushkar, Kishangarh and nearby Rajasthan farming conditions.

Practical Execution

Plans are made for field use, with attention to budget, labour, water and market reality.

Full-Cycle Support

Consulting covers setup, production, plant care, harvesting, marketing and finance planning.
